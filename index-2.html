About Us

expressminning is an independent, privately-owned fee-only investment management firm based in the Flatiron District, New York City. Since 2008, we have partnered with leading companies such as Testimonial, SpaceX, and Tesla. We have expanded our global presence with offices in Turkey, Russia, Australia, South Africa, the United States, Egypt, and Dubai, UAE. We provide a unique blend of financial planning and investment management expertise, offering the convenience of a full-service financial resource while maintaining the independence needed to align fully with our clients' interests.

expressminning offers both long-term and short-term investment opportunities. The firm started with trading stocks, shares, and bonds, moved into forex trading in 2010, and quickly adapted to emerging markets, becoming one of the pioneers in cryptocurrency trading with the advent of Bitcoin in 2010. Our strategic partnerships with SpaceX have also allowed us to explore investment avenues in the aerospace sector, while our collaboration with Tesla opens up opportunities in sustainable energy and electric vehicles.
